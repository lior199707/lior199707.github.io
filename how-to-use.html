Settings

Used For

The settings are used to define different tips distribution systems.
after filling the settings and pressing the save button it will be saved so each time you access the page the settingd will be loaded automatically.

How To Use

After each settings change click the save button below the settings otherwise the new settings won't apply.

Waiters Calculator

Used For

The "Waiters Calculator" can be used in restaurants that work on a mutual tips/tips sharing system.

Calculates the amount of money that each role earned based on the settings you defined , deducts it from the total tips and distribute the remaining money between the servers by their work hours.

How To Use

If you want to distribute the money equaly between the staff (according to their hours of work) set all settings to 0 and add all the employees to the waiters table.

Percentage Calculator

Used For

The "Percentage Calculator" can be used in restaurants that work on a mutual tips/tips sharing system and waiters have different tips percentage
(For example in sheff restaurants new waiters earn 70% of the per hour earnings and senior waiters earn 100%)

Calculates the amount of money that each role earned based on the settings you defined , deducts it from the total tips and distribute the remaining money between the servers by their work hours and the percentage set to them.

How To Use

Staff Calculator

Used For

The "Staff Calculator" can be used in restaurants that work on a mutual tips/tips sharing system, each staff role(except waiters) has a specific percentage of the total tips.
The calculator presents the revenue per role per person in said role based on their hours of work.
For example lets say a restaurant has 3 runners and the runners get 10% of total tips, lets say the total tips are 1000$ so the runners have earned 150$ in tip, the calculator will distribute the money between the runners according to their hours of work.

Calculates the salary each role earned based on the settings you defined , calculates the per hour earnings of each role by dividing the total role revenue by the total role hours, and calculates how much an employee gets by multyplying the per hour earnings by the hours of work.

How To Use

Points Calculator

Used For

The "Points Calculator" can be used in restaurants that share their tips between employees by assigning each employee a certain number of points which his daily tips earnings will be calculated by.
This calculator ignores the settings

Takes the total amount of tips divides it by the total sum of the employees' points in order to find the per point revenue.
Calculates employees daily salary by multiplying the per point revenue by the employee's points.
